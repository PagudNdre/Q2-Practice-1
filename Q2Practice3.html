Synopsis


Haikyuu!! follows Shōyō Hinata, a short but determined volleyball player inspired by the legendary “Little Giant.” After losing badly to the gifted setter Tobio Kageyama in middle school, Hinata enters Karasuno High only to discover Kageyama is now his teammate. The two form a powerful but initially rocky partnership, combining Hinata’s speed with Kageyama’s precision. As Karasuno rebuilds from its reputation as “The Fallen Crows,” the team trains intensely and faces strong rivals like Nekoma, Aoba Johsai, and Shiratorizawa. Through dramatic matches and personal growth, each player learns to overcome weaknesses and trust one another. The story culminates in Karasuno reaching the national stage, where Hinata and Kageyama continue striving toward their dreams.
Character Description Appearance
Eren Eren Yeager is the main protagonist of Attack on Titan. He begins as a determined and impulsive boy who dreams of freedom beyond the Walls. After witnessing his mother’s death, he becomes fiercely driven to destroy the Titans. Over time, Eren evolves into a complex, morally ambiguous figure whose pursuit of freedom leads him down a darker, world-shaping path.
Mikasa Mikasa Ackerman is one of the central characters in Attack on Titan. She’s calm, disciplined, and exceptionally skilled in combat—widely regarded as one of humanity’s strongest soldiers. Deeply loyal to Eren, she’s driven by a fierce protectiveness rooted in their shared past. Beneath her stoic exterior, Mikasa carries a quiet emotional depth and a longing for a peaceful life with the people she loves.
Commander Erwin Commander Erwin Smith is the calculating and charismatic leader of the Scout Regiment in Attack on Titan. Known for his unwavering resolve and strategic brilliance, he’s willing to make immense sacrifices—including of his own soldiers and himself—for the advancement of humanity’s knowledge and the hope of a better future. Erwin carries a heavy burden of guilt, but his unshakeable determination and ability to inspire others make him one of the series’ most respected and iconic figures.
